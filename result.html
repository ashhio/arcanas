Meanings:

To interpret your card:

1. Pick a meaning that stands out the most to you.
2. Apply the meaning to yourself as best you can.
3. Ask yourself what your interpretation says about you and/or what actions you can take based on your interpretation.

Example interpretation:
Card: 10 of Wands
Meanings: Overextending, Burdens, Struggle
Chosen meaning: Burdens
Intepretation: “I never have time to relax because I have lots of work.”
Action: “I should make time to relax today.”
